# Power BI Build Plan

## 1. National Medicaid Office Access Map

- Map Medicaid office point locations across the United States.
- Show state-level office counts and office density measures.
- Include filters for state, region, rurality, and data source notes as available.

## 2. State Comparison

- Allow comparison of two states.
- Compare office counts, county coverage, office density, and rural-urban distribution.
- Highlight counties with limited office access relative to planned need indicators.

## 3. County Access Gaps

- Map counties with zero or low Medicaid office availability.
- Display county-level access context such as poverty, no-vehicle households, broadband access, and rurality.
- Include ranked county tables and tooltip details.

## 4. Access Barrier Index

- Present a transparent county-level index combining office supply, geographic access, need, transportation barriers, and digital access barriers.
- Categorize counties into concern levels after the index methodology is finalized.
- Provide slicers and explanatory tooltips for index components.

## 5. Data Notes and Limitations

- Document source dates, data coverage, and refresh assumptions.
- State limitations around causal interpretation, point-in-time office data, county-level aggregation, and missing non-office enrollment supports.
- Provide clear caveats for portfolio and policy-facing interpretation.
